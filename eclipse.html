The difference between a lunar eclipse and a solar eclipse Here on earth, We can experience two types of eclipses: Solar eclipse and lunar eclipse. Solar eclipses occur when the moon passes between the sun and earth, causing the moon to cast a shadow on the earth. A Lunar eclipse occurs when Earth is between the sun and the moon, causing Earth to cast a shadow on the moon.
